Running the activity:

Candy love is a great team building activity that gets the participants talking about their life beyond the work activities

  1. Place a package of M&Ms, Skittles or another colorful candy in a jar
  2. Ask a participant to pick a candy from the jar, and then share something about him/herself according to the candy color:
